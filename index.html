About the Factorio Power Planner

A free, browser-based power calculator for Factorio 2.x, updated for the Space Age expansion and its quality tiers. It turns a reactor layout — or a target power figure — into the exact machine counts you need, with no over-provisioning, across three technologies: nuclear, fusion, and solar. Every base number comes straight from the game's prototype data (data.raw), so the ratios match what you build in-game.

Nuclear reactor ratios

Draw a layout on the reactor grid and the planner sizes the heat exchangers, steam turbines, offshore pumps, and fuel-cell consumption. Each reactor makes its base 40 MW of heat plus +100% for every directly adjacent reactor (the neighbour bonus), so a 2×2 block produces 4 × 40 × (1 + 2) = 480 MW and needs 48 heat exchangers and 83 steam turbines at normal quality. Because quality scales every machine equally, an all-legendary build uses the same machine counts as an all-normal one but outputs 2.5× the power.

Fusion reactor neighbour bonus and staggered layouts

Fusion links differently from nuclear: reactors connect through shared fluid connections, two per side, so offsetting a row by one tile lets each side reach two different neighbours. A staggered fusion array therefore hits the in-game +500% neighbour bonus, versus +400% for a parallel block. The free-form layout editor places reactors as 2×2 tokens, reads each one's links from real tile adjacency, and flags any reactor boxed in on all sides (an inserter can't reach it to load fusion power cells). It also sizes the fusion generators and the cryogenic plants that re-cool the fluoroketone loop.

Solar panel to accumulator ratios by planet

Solar is a per-surface planner: the exact solar panel : accumulator ratio for every planet and space-platform orbit, at any quality mix. On Nauvis the exact ratio is 2646:3125 — about 0.85 accumulators per panel, or a simple 13:11. That is not the old 21:25 figure, because Factorio 2.0 lengthened the day to seven minutes. Enter a target average power to turn the per-MW ratios into concrete panel and accumulator counts.

FAQ

How many heat exchangers and turbines does a nuclear reactor need?

It depends on the neighbour bonus, not the reactor count alone — which is why the planner works from your actual layout. A 2×2 block (480 MW) needs 48 heat exchangers and 83 steam turbines at normal quality.

Does staggering fusion reactors actually help?

Yes. A parallel block caps each interior reactor at +400%; staggering reaches the +500% practical maximum, so the same reactor count makes more power. Use the Fill parallel and Fill staggered buttons on the Fusion tab to compare.

Is it accurate for the Space Age quality system?

Yes — each machine stat scales by the correct per-tier multiplier (+30% per level, +150% at legendary), including the accumulator's special +100%-per-level capacity.

Which Factorio version is supported?

Factorio 2.0.7 and later. Version 2.0.7 changed the water-to-steam ratio to 1:10, which the offshore-pump math relies on.
